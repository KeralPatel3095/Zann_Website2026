digital scanner integration

connect your systems to Zann

Register the lab in your scanner software, route cases with the right account IDs, and fall back to open STL when portals are not an option.

connect your scanner

Add Zann Dental Laboratory through each manufacturer's workflow. On iTero, use Zann lab account #64717 where prompted. For Carestream, Dentsply Sirona, and 3Shape, use "add lab / collaborator" and select Zann so outbound scans land in our queue. cadcamdesign@zanndental.com

the STL advantage

Export open STL bundles per the software IFU, then transmit with WeTransfer or your HIPAA-reviewed channel. This path keeps you moving when you need a neutral archive outside a closed manufacturer network - or when referring practices standardize on STL handoff.

hybrid workflow support

from impressions to archived STL

Practices moving from physical models to a fully digital record stay supported end to end.

stone to STL conversion

For offices still relying on PVS and poured models, we provide a digitization service. Physical models are scanned and converted to secure STL archives for planning, long-term storage, and predictable lab dialogue.
