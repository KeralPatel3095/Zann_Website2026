Digital Workflow

modern digital workflows designed for speed, precision, and seamless communication between clinic and lab.

Send a case

Zann is

modern digital workflows

we support digital impressions and scan-based case submissions to simplify workflows and improve consistency. from single units to complex restorative and removable cases, digital files allow efficient communication and accurate fabrication.

precision without compromise

digital scans reduce distortion associated with traditional materials and provide detailed records for design and manufacturing. this supports precise margins, predictable fit, and smoother case progression from submission to delivery.

you get consistent quality whether the case is fully digital or hybrid. Every case is checked before shipment.

integrated workflows

we work with leading intraoral scanners and accept a wide range of open-file submissions. digital cases can be reviewed quickly, with clear communication if additional information is needed.

From single crowns to full-arch and implant cases - we deliver restorations through a digital workflow that meets the demands of everyday practice.

digital workflow that fits your practice

We accept digital scans and design files for crowns, bridges, implants, dentures, and more. Our design and fabrication steps are aligned so we can go from your scan or file to a finished restoration with the same quality we deliver for traditional cases. Whether you're fully digital or adding digital cases gradually, we work with your file types and timelines.

You get restorations that fit your case and your workflow. We're here to support your next digital case.

our commitment to quality

our technicians combine digital design expertise with proven laboratory craftsmanship. whether a case is fully digital or supported by conventional records, every restoration follows the same commitment to quality and fit.

tell us what you need

send your scan, restoration prescription, shade details, bite records, and any notes or photos. if you have design preferences, let us know - or our team can develop the case from scratch based on your clinical goals.

What our digital workflow includes

Scan submission & import

Send digital scans in the file formats we support. We import into our design environment so your case is built from accurate 3D data from the start.

01

Digital design (CAD)

Restorations are designed with attention to margins, occlusion, and aesthetics. We work to your specs and restoration type so the design is ready for fabrication.

02

CAM milling & 3D printing

We use milling and 3D printing as appropriate for each case - crowns, bridges, implant parts, models, guides, and appliances - so output matches the design.

03

Implant planning support

For implant cases we can work from your planning data to produce surgical guides and restorative components that fit your chosen system and plan.

04

Digital dentures & try-ins

Digital denture workflows from scan to try-in and final delivery. We produce try-in bases and finished dentures using digital design and additive or traditional fabrication.

05

Quality check & delivery

Every restoration is checked for fit, occlusion, and finish before shipment. We deliver on the timeline we quote so your digital workflow stays predictable.

06

did you know?

digital workflows can reduce remakes, improve turnaround times, and make case communication more efficient. accurate scans also create valuable records for future reference or replacement cases.

at Zann, we run our digital workflow to the same quality standards as the rest of our lab so you get predictable results.
