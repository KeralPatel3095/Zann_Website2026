Flippers

Temporary removable partials that replace one or a few missing teeth. Lightweight, aesthetic, and quick to deliver - ideal while healing or waiting for an implant or bridge.

Zann is

high performance flippers that work.

Our flippers are temporary acrylic partials designed to replace one or several missing teeth. They're lightweight, easy to wear, and built for aesthetics so patients can smile with confidence while healing or before a permanent restoration. We match shade and contour for a natural look and deliver with a quick turnaround.

Lightweight fit & aesthetics

A flipper is a thin, acrylic partial that fills the gap left by missing teeth. We focus on comfortable retention, minimal bulk, and natural-looking teeth and gingiva so the appliance is easy to wear and looks good when the patient smiles. Flippers are intended for temporary use while planning implants, bridges, or other definitive care.

You get an appliance that seats cleanly and blends with the smile. Every flipper is checked for fit and shade before shipment.

materials & fabrication

We fabricate flippers from quality acrylic with attention to thin, lightweight design and shade-matched teeth. Our workflow supports quick turnaround so patients get their temporary replacement soon after extractions or when planning definitive treatment. Each flipper is trimmed and polished for comfort and a natural appearance.

From single-tooth to multi-tooth spaces - we deliver flippers that meet the demands of temporary tooth replacement.

temporary tooth replacement

Flippers are ideal when a patient has one or a few teeth missing and is waiting for an implant, bridge, or other definitive restoration. They restore appearance and support confidence without the cost or complexity of a permanent partial. We build each flipper for comfortable retention and natural look; they're not intended for long-term heavy chewing.

You get a temporary solution that looks good and is easy to wear. We follow consistent protocols so every flipper meets our lab standards.

our commitment to quality

Every flipper case is handled with clear communication and a focus on quick turnaround. We use quality acrylic and proven fabrication so the appliance fits well and matches the requested shade. From single-tooth to multi-tooth spaces, we aim for accurate fit and on-time delivery.

tell us what you need

Send your impression and let us know which teeth to replace, tooth shade and mold preference, and whether it's for post-extraction or pre-restoration. We'll fabricate a flipper that fits your patient's space and aesthetic needs.

did you know?

A flipper gives patients an aesthetic solution right after an extraction or while waiting for an implant or bridge. Because it's temporary and acrylic, it's not designed for years of heavy use - but for the in-between period, it helps maintain appearance and patient confidence without a big investment.

At Zann, we craft each flipper for comfortable fit and natural look so you get a reliable temporary option and happier patients.
