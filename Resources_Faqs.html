At a glance

Policy, logistics, and escalation paths your team can rely on when patients ask - or when a case needs a decision before it hits the lab.

01 - CDCP

Does ZANN Dental Lab accept CDCP cases?

Yes, we are fully equipped to support clinicians participating in the Canadian Dental Care Plan (CDCP) across Ontario with compliant removable prosthetic solutions.

02 - warranty

What is the warranty on your restorations?

We offer a “ZANN Integrity Guarantee” covering manufacturing defects or material failure under normal use within the listed warranty periods.

ZANN integrity guarantee - summary
  • Crown, bridge & veneers5 years
  • Dentures2 years
  • Nightguards / splints3 - 6 months
  • Repairs & relines3 months
