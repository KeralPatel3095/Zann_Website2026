Case Submission Guide

Case Submission

Welcome to Zann Dental Lab's case submission guide. We're here to help you send cases accurately and efficiently so you get predictable turnaround and quality.

This page explains how to prepare your case, choose a submission method (digital or physical), use our pick-up schedule, package and ship correctly, and what to expect from receipt through delivery. We accept crowns, bridges, dentures, implants, night guards, sports guards, orthodontics, retainers, bleaching trays, and more.

Guide

Step-by-Step Case Submission

Prepare accurately, choose the right submission method, use our pickup schedule when needed, and ship/package properly so we can process your case without delay.

01

Prepare Your Case

Before you submit, gather everything so nothing is missing when we receive your case or files.

  • Patient information: full name, age, contact number, and any special instructions.
  • Prescription form: tooth numbers, materials, shade, and special notes.
  • Digital files: intraoral scans (STL/PLY/OBJ) and bite registration where applicable.
  • Physical models/impressions: label patient name + tooth numbers, and pack securely.
Tip: Double-check tooth numbers and shade before sending.
02

Digital Submission

Use our secure portal to upload scans and attach the prescription form.

  • Upload all files in one submission for a complete set.
  • Attach the prescription form (PDF or image) for material, shade, and notes.
  • Supported formats: STL, PLY, OBJ. Contact us if your file format differs.
03

Physical Submission (Pickup Available)

Choose pickup or drop-off based on what works best for your clinic.

  • Call to arrange pickup time. We confirm the window and requirements.
  • Have the case ready at the agreed time.
  • Use clear labeling (patient name + restoration type) so the case is identifiable.
  • Prefer drop-off? Bring it during business hours.
04

Send It Complete

Proper labeling and complete information help us process your case without delays.

  • Label everything clearly: patient name, tooth numbers, restoration type.
  • Include the prescription form inside the package.
  • One package per case (or per clearly labeled patient package) to avoid mix-ups.

Prepare once, send with confidence

Have your prescription, patient details, and models or digital files ready. Clear labeling and complete information help us turn your case around without delays.

Instructions

Packaging Instructions

Proper packaging protects your case in transit and helps us process it quickly. Follow these guidelines whether you use pickup or ship it yourself.

  • Wrap securely and keep items stable so impressions/models don't shift or break. Use bubble wrap or foam and a sturdy box.
  • Label clearly + include the prescription (patient name, tooth numbers, restoration type) with labels on the outside and inside, and place the prescription form in the package.
Reminder: One package per case helps avoid mix-ups and speeds up intake.

Shipping

Shipping and Confirmation

We work with standard/express couriers and international carriers so you can choose the right speed. When you ship (or when we arrange pickup), you'll get confirmation and tracking where applicable.

  • Choose the right courier option (standard or express). For time-sensitive cases, ask about same-day or next-day availability.
  • Confirm and track: use the tracking number when provided, and after we receive your case we'll confirm receipt, assign a case number, and share an estimated turnaround time.

At the lab

Case Processing & Updates

After we receive your case, we review the prescription and models/digital files and move them through our workflow. If anything is unclear, we'll reach out to avoid delays.

  • Prescription and model review: We check prescription completeness and match it to models or digital files. Missing info or unclear notes may trigger a call/email.
  • Digital + CAD workflow: For digital cases, we import scans and design in CAD. If approval is needed, we send designs for your review.
  • Physical model quality: Models are scanned and checked for fit/accuracy before proceeding.
  • Clear communication: Questions about shade, design, or case details are communicated promptly.

Quality

Quality Check & Delivery

Every case goes through quality control before it leaves the lab. We verify fit, finish, and shade consistency so the restoration meets our standards.

  • Quality control: We check fit, margins, occlusion (where applicable), finish, and shade against your prescription.
  • Delivery options: Standard or express delivery based on your choice and location.
  • Receipt check: When you receive the case, inspect it and contact us within 48 hours for anything unclear.

After delivery

Follow-Up & Feedback

Your feedback helps us improve. After delivery, review the restoration and report any adjustments or issues promptly.

  • Review fit & aesthetics: Note adjustments (bite, contour, shade).
  • Report within 48 hours: Contact us quickly so we can resolve promptly.

Tips

Additional Tips & Notes

Small details make a big difference in smooth case flow. Keep these in mind when preparing and submitting.

  • Double-check prescription forms before sending. Missing tooth numbers/shade/material can delay the case.
  • Mark shade and material clearly on the prescription, and on the case/model when possible.
  • Submit digital files together so the case is complete in one upload.
  • Use one clearly labeled package when sending multiple restorations for the same case/patient.
Need help? If you're unsure about packaging, file formats, or pickup times, call us. We'll walk you through the process.
