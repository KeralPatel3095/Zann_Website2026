Zirconia Veneer

high-strength zirconia veneers for a durable, beautiful smile.

Zann is

high performance zirconia veneers that work.

our zirconia veneers are milled from premium high-translucency zirconia using advanced cad/cam workflows. the result is a precise, durable restoration with clean margins, natural contours, and modern aesthetics.

Strength & aesthetics

zirconia veneers combine exceptional strength with improved translucency in a metal-free form. high-translucency and layered options allow for natural depth, balanced brightness, and seamless shade integration.

You get a durable, aesthetic solution for smile design. Every veneer is checked for margin fit, contour, and shade before shipment.

premium materials & CAD/CAM precision

we use premium medical-grade zirconia blocks from trusted manufacturers, processed through calibrated milling and sintering cycles. this ensures consistent strength, precision fit, and aesthetic performance across every case - from single units to full smile rehabilitations.

each veneer is milled and finished for accurate fit, lifelike contour, and harmonious smile design.

the power of zirconia veneers

each veneer follows controlled digital design, milling, and finishing workflows. this supports clean margins, predictable seating, and restorations that require minimal adjustment at insertion.

We follow strict protocols for design, milling, and sintering so every veneer meets our lab standards for margin integrity and esthetics.

our commitment to quality

our technicians are trained in digital anterior design, finishing, and detailed characterization. with clear communication and dependable turnaround times, you can expect veneers that integrate naturally and perform reliably.

find the right shade for your smile

a full range of shades and custom characterizations are available to match surrounding dentition. provide your preferred shade, photos, or reference notes - we'll match it.

did you know?

zirconia veneers have become a popular option for cases requiring added strength without sacrificing aesthetics. high-translucency materials and layered techniques now allow zirconia to achieve natural-looking anterior results with excellent durability.

at Zann, we craft each zirconia veneer with attention to fit, contour, and shade so you get beautiful, lasting results and happier patients.
