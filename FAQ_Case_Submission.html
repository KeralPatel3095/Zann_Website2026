General FAQ

common case submission questions

We accept crowns, bridges, implant restorations, dentures, guards, aligner-related appliances, and other standard lab cases for both digital and physical workflows.

Submit complete prescription details, accurate patient name, and all required files/models in one submission package to prevent clarification delays.

Yes, hybrid cases are possible when clearly labeled. Include notes that explain which components are digital and which are physical.

Use your primary lab contact or support line and include patient name, case type, and due date for fastest response.

Digital FAQ

digital submission questions

These are the most common digital case questions from clinics before upload.

file formats and scan quality

  • accepted formats usually include STL, PLY, and OBJ.
  • ensure margins are visible and scan holes are minimized.
  • attach bite and opposing scans when required.

upload and naming standards

  • use consistent naming: patientName_tooth_caseType.
  • upload all files and prescription together in one case.
  • include design notes for contacts, occlusion, and shade.

Physical FAQ

physical submission questions

01 packing

protect models/impressions to avoid transit distortion and fit issues.

02 labeling

label outside and inside with patient name and case type for clean intake.

03 documents

place the prescription form in a dry sleeve inside the package.

04 dispatch

share courier tracking and keep one patient case per labeled package.
