technical

how to send a case digitally

Connect through your preferred scanning platform using the details below. ZANN Dental Lab supports a fully digital ecosystem.

digital scanner integration

  • iTero: Call 1-800-577-8767 (Option 1 twice). Provide ZANN Dental's phone number (416-696-5454) or account number (64717).
  • 3Shape TRIOS: Find us on 3Shape Communicate using cadcamdesign@zanndental.com.
  • Medit Link: Connect via the Medit Link portal using cadcamdesign@zanndental.com.
  • Sirona Connect: Add "ZANN Dental" or "ZANN Dental Lab" as your favorite lab.
  • Carestream: Add "ZANN Dental Laboratory" on CS Connect.
  • Other (3M, E4D, STL): Export as an STL file and send via WeTransfer to cadcamdesign@zanndental.com.

technical

physical impressions & models

When digital isn't in-office yet, ship models or impressions - we convert physical work into the same archival-grade STL files your digital cases use.

traditional impression conversion

  • Stone to STL: We offer model conversion services. Send physical models or PVS impressions, and our technicians will convert them into high-resolution digital files for secure archiving.
