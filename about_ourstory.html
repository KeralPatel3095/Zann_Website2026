about ZANN dental laboratory

a legacy of precision and craft

ZANN is a third-generation dental laboratory defined by consistency, refinement, and trust. Over the past 30+ years, we have expanded from a small, family-run lab into a progressive, technology-driven operation - while maintaining the attention to detail that has always set our work apart.

the generations behind the craft
01 evolution

heritage craftsmanship to next-generation technology

What began as a commitment to heritage craftsmanship has evolved into a next-generation dental technology corporation specializing in digital prosthetics and advanced materials.

Today, we integrate decades of hands-on experience with leading digital workflows to support clinicians with consistency, efficiency, and precision.
