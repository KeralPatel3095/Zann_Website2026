Advanced / Custom Implant Restorations

Full-arch restorations, custom bars, hybrid prostheses, and complex implant cases. Designed and fabricated for your most demanding patients.

Zann is

advanced and custom implant restorations that work.

Our advanced implant offering includes full-arch fixed restorations, custom titanium and zirconia bars, hybrid prostheses, implant-supported overdentures, and multi-unit bridges. We use digital planning and proven materials to deliver restorations that fit your implant layout and restore function and aesthetics for the long term.

Full-arch & custom design

Advanced implant cases demand precise passive fit across multiple implants and careful design of emergence and occlusion. We fabricate custom bars and frameworks that align with your implant positions and restorative plan. Full-arch fixed and hybrid restorations are designed for strength, retrievability, and natural appearance.

You get a restoration that fits your case design and delivers predictable function and aesthetics. Every framework and prosthesis is verified for fit and occlusion before shipment.

digital planning & premium materials

We use digital scans and planning to design custom bars, frameworks, and full-arch restorations. Titanium and zirconia frameworks are milled for accuracy; PMMA and definitive materials are chosen for each phase. Our workflows support immediate-load, staged, and conventional protocols so your advanced cases are handled with consistency and care.

From full-arch fixed to implant overdentures and multi-unit custom bridges - we deliver advanced implant work that meets the highest lab standards.

full-arch & hybrid restorations

Our advanced implant offering includes full-arch fixed restorations (screw-retained or hybrid), custom milled titanium and zirconia bars, and implant-supported overdentures. We work from your scan or cast and implant positions to design frameworks that achieve passive fit and support the final prosthesis. Angled implants and multi-unit spans are accommodated with custom abutments and bar design.

We follow strict protocols for framework fit, occlusion, and material selection so every advanced case meets our lab standards.

our commitment to quality

Every advanced implant case is handled with clear communication and careful planning. We verify implant positions and use quality components and materials. From try-in to final delivery, we aim for accuracy and predictability so your patients get lasting, functional results.

partner with us on complex cases

Send your full-arch or multi-unit implant case with scans, implant system and positions, and your preferred design (fixed, hybrid, overdenture). We'll work with you to deliver a custom restoration that fits your plan and your patient's needs.

did you know?

Full-arch and hybrid implant restorations require precise framework fit and careful design to avoid stress on implants and ensure patient comfort. At Zann, we use digital design and milling to deliver custom bars and restorations that fit your implant layout and support long-term success.

We craft each advanced implant restoration with attention to fit, occlusion, and aesthetics so you get predictable outcomes and happier patients.
